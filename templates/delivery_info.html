Delivery Information

Delivery Times

We aim to deliver your orders as quickly as possible. Standard delivery times are:

  • Local deliveries: 1-2 business days
  • Regional deliveries: 3-5 business days

Delivery Charges

Our delivery charges are calculated based on the location and weight of your order. Standard charges are:

  • Orders below $50: $5.00 delivery charge
  • Orders above $50: Free delivery

Returns and Refunds

If there are any issues with your order, such as missing items or damage during delivery, please contact our support team within 48 hours of receiving your order. We will assist you in processing returns or refunds as necessary.
